Power BI Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze business performance, customer behavior, product sales, and order/invoice trends. The dashboard helps businesses understand sales patterns, customer distribution, and product performance through data visualization.

The dashboard is divided into four major analytical sections to provide a complete business overview.

Dashboard Sections

Business Overview

This section provides a high-level summary of the business performance.

Key metrics include:

* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Total Quantity Sold

Visualizations include:

* Monthly sales trends
* Sales per customer vs average order value
* Total sales by country
* Customer distribution by country

This helps identify **top performing regions and sales patterns over time**.
Product Insights

This section analyzes product-level performance.

Key insights include:

* Total sales by product description
* Quantity sold by product
* Product-wise sales contribution
* Detailed product table with stock code, quantity, and revenue

These visuals help identify **top-selling products and product demand trends**.
Customer Analytics

This section focuses on customer behavior and demographics.

Key metrics include:

* Total customers
* Total orders
* Total sales
* Total quantity purchased

Visualizations include:

* Customer distribution by country
* Monthly customer growth
* Country-wise customer analysis
* Average order value vs sales per customer

This helps understand **customer engagement and regional customer distribution**.

Order & Invoice Analysis

This section focuses on transaction-level insights.

Key metrics include:

* Total invoices
* Total orders
* Total sales
* Average order value

Visualizations include:

* Total orders and sales by quarter
* Monthly invoice trends
* Invoice distribution by country
* Order value by invoice

This helps track **order frequency and revenue patterns over time**.
Key Features

* Interactive Power BI dashboard
* Country-based filtering
* Customer ID filtering
* Date range analysis
* Multi-page analytical reports
* Business, product, and customer insights in one platform

Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop**
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
